# The Grand Pythagorean Quest (Difficulty: Easy)

A **Pythagorean triplet** is a set of three natural numbers, a < b < c, for which:

$$
a^{2} + b^{2} = c^{2}
$$

**Example:**

$$
3^2 + 4^2 = 9 + 16 = 25 = 5^2
$$
